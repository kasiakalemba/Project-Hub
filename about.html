Here is a little about me.




Researcher & Data Scientist.

My name is Katarzyna (or Kasia for short) and I am a researcher. I have a Master's Degree in Biomedical Science and a Certificate in Data Science, both from Rutgers University in New Brunswick, New Jersey.

My daily research in the lab focuses what goes wrong on a molecular level in Type II Diabetes. I also study differences between how gender affects gluconeogenesis, or simply the way our body makes sugar when it runs out.

I offer strong leadership and mentoring expertise, a publication track record with a solid understanding of molecular biology, experimental design and successful project execution. On the data science side, I am proficient in Python, Javascript, SQL, Big Data, Machine Learning and in tools such as Pandas, Matplotlib, Tableau, or Leaflet. I'm an excellent communicator, enthusiastic team player, and a creative thinker.

I'm open to joining a team of driven individuals who display passion, problem-solving skills and desire for company growth. I hope to thrive in a stimulating environment that combines biological and health science with data science. I'm excited to bring my expertise to your table.
